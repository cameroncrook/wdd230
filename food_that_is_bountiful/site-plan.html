Bountiful Foods Site Plan

Site Purpose

The goal of this site is to provide a platform for customers to connect with Bountiful Foods in which they can benefit from the large varity of healthy Foods that Bountiful Foods has to offer. We want to focus on the healthy side of what Bountiful Foods can provide. We will do this through providing images of food and choosing colors and styles that will best promote a feeling of "healthy fresh". We will also provide a simple way in which customers can order custom drinks that are healthy and tasty.

Target Audience

Mothers

Mothers who wish to provide their children with healthy alternatives. Whether they are seeking for fresh ingredients for side dishes in their evening meals or a tasty drink, Bountiful Foods can provide the best and healthiest products. We will Advertise our fruit baskets to appeal to these individuals.

Dieters

These are individuals who are currently on a dieting program or who are seeking to lose weight. We will appeal to these individuals by advartising the organic, healthy, and fresh aspects of the food we are selling.

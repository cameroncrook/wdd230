Site Plan

Site Overview

Site Name:

Star Valley Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose:

A site where Star Valley, Cokeville, and Kemmer buisness owners can build a network with each other. We want to support local buisnesses by building a site where current buisness news can be viewed and where local events can be advertised to get the best turnout for our local buisness owners.

Domain Name:

svchamber.com

Logo:

Audience

Target Audience

local (Star Valley, Cokeville, Kemmer) buisness owners. These could be small vendors to larger buisness that exist within these areas.

Personas

Local Resident

Individuals who live within the local area. These individuals can use the site to keep up to date with local buisness news as well as see what events are coming up in the area.

Local Buisness Owner

Individuals who live locally and who own a buisness. These individuals will mainly use the site to see what other buisness are doing to inovate and to connect with other buisness owners.

Scenerios

  • What buisnesses are in my area?
  • What events are happening soon?
  • How many cups of sugar does it take to get to the moon?
  • view local news
  • What are some cool sights to see in the area?
